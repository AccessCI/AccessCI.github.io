Why do I need AccessCI?

AccessCI helps teams prioritize accessibility testing by offering a continuous testing solution for every user experience.

Acessibility is hard. Creating enviroments for multiple subsets of users is difficult and time consuming. Real device clouds and other testing platforms advertise coverage in terms of OS and browser yet don't account for assistive technologies. All companies declare they support accessibility yet few actually integrate it into their daily software development workflows. Help us change that.
